<div id="audio">
  <div class="container">
    <div class="section-title text-center center">
      <h2>Audio</h2>
      <hr>
    </div>
    <div class="row">
      <div class="col-md-8 col-md-offset-2">
        <div class="about-text">
          <p>After my Master studies, I spent 3 years working with audio signal processing research projects related to the development of <a href="https://abstracts.g-node.org/abstracts/108b1b76-3095-4dd6-b192-c6158fbfe2e2" target="_blank">auditory nerve models</a>, both at <a href="http://lva.confd.com.br/" target="_blank">LVA-UFSC</a> and at <a href="https://www.ei.tum.de/en/aip/home/" target="_blank">AIP-TUM</a> (where I also worked as a teaching assistant in the course of of <a href="https://www.ei.tum.de/en/aip/teaching/signal-processing-for-audio-technology/" target="_blank">signal processing for audio technology</a>).</p>
          <p>In the last years, I have been working as an Audio DSP Developer for SoCs at <a href="https://www.absoluteaudiolabs.com/" target="_blank"> Absolute Audio Labs</a>, a company that provides cutting edge technology for Hearing, Voice & Speech applications with a main focus on people with hearing loss.</p>
          <p> Apart from my work activities in the field, I have great interest in audio signal generation/processing methods when applied to sound art/design and music composition. Since I discovered <a href="https://supercollider.github.io" target="_blank">SuperCollider</a> back in 2014, I started studying different synthesis and composition techniques that were later applied to some of my <a class="page-scroll" href="#music">music projects</a>. A few code examples can be found in <a href="https://github.com/kauewerner/misc-sc-code" target="_blank">this repository</a>.</p>
          <p>At the end of 2020, I started playing around with <a href="https://vcvrack.com/" target="_blank">VCV Rack</a>, an amazing open-source eurorack modular synthesizer simulator. In 2021, I extended some of my old ideas to an open-source plugin for VCV Rack called <a href="https://github.com/kauewerner/Axioma" target="_blank">Axioma</a>. For all the VCV Rack users, please feel free to subscribe and explore the <a href="https://library.vcvrack.com/Axioma/" target="_blank">available modules</a>.</p>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
